General Code of Conduct

This Code of conduct has been adapted from the PuPPy Code of Conduct as well as the PyCon Code of Conduct

PuPPy US is a community local user group intended for networking and collaboration in the developer community. The PuPPy Mentorship Application was designed to enable development of further connections and relationships between PuPPy community members. As such, users of this application agree to abide by the PuPPy community code of conduct.

We value the participation of each member of the Python community and want all users of the PuPPy Mentorship App to have an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. Accordingly, all users are expected to show respect and courtesy to other users across the application, and at all local user group events, whether officially sponsored by PuPPy or not.

To make clear what is expected, all users, collaborators, and volunteers for this platform are required to conform to the following Code of Conduct. Maintainers will enforce this code throughout use of this platform.

Our Standards

PuPPy is dedicated to providing a positive experience for everyone, regardless of age, gender identity and expression, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, ethnicity, nationality, race, or religion (or lack thereof), education, or socio-economic status.

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

  • Being kind to others
  • Behaving professionally
  • Using welcoming and inclusive language
  • Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
  • Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
  • Focusing on what is best for the community
  • Showing empathy towards other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

  • Harassment of other users in any form
  • Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
  • Violent threats or language directed against another person
  • Sexual language and imagery shared in communications with other users
  • Insults or put downs
  • Sexist, racist, xenophobic, homophobic, transphobic, ableist, or exclusionary jokes
  • Excessive swearing
  • Unwelcome sexual attention or advances
  • Unwelcome physical contact
  • Other conduct that is inappropriate for a professional community group including people of many different backgrounds

If a user engages in behavior that violates this code of conduct, the PuPPy Mentorship App maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warning the offender or expulsion from the application.
